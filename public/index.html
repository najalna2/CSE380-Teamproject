"Road To The Show"
"Game Design Document"

by Dongha Song, Hangjoon Cho, Yeonsik Kang

INTRODUCTION:

His job is to knock out all the other baseball players and claim the victory for himself. He will use physical means to defeat them. His main weapon is his favorite baseball bat, which won't break until all the other baseball players are down. However, the other players won't stand still and watch Player take them down. Before they can take down the main character, Player will have to take them all down first.

TECHNOLOGY:

Road To The Show is a 2d side scrolling game. The game's construction will be developed for the windows platform using wolfie2d, an engine developed by Richard McKenna. Sound effects will be. Artwork is developed using piskel and krita.

BACK STORY:

The main character “X” became a baseball player with the determination to win the league. However, he realized that he could never win the league with his skills. He was always fourth. he thought "I want to be number one too. But since I don't have the ability to do that, wouldn't it be possible for me to become number one if there were no superiors?" So he decided to do it.

OBJECTIVE:

Major League Baseball is the world famous baseball league that many baseball players desire to be the top player. However, succeeding in MLB is the hardest goal to achieve as a baseball player. Here, the player is a novice hitter, and wishes to be a world famous player. Currently, the No.1 player is Ohtani, and defeating him is inevitable to be the top player. The player will continue to challenge and encounter Ohtani in the field. It will be a tough way till beating him. Many baseball players will disturb his road to the show, but he will not give up his dream and continue his road.

GAMEPLAY:

The game will work like a side scroller, with the user character able to run left and right, and jump up or down onto platforms and other surfaces as controlled. The task would be instructed at every stage of the game. For the most part, it is a dodging game. The enemy characters, the athletes would attack in different styles with their own personal characteristics. In the middle of dodging, the user should find a way to attack the enemy character and knock them down to proceed to the next stage.

CONTROLS:

This game will be played using both a keyboard and mouse. Once started, use the following:
  • A : Auto Attack
  • Left Arrow: Move Left
  • RIGHT-ARROW - Move Right
  • SPACE OR UPPER-ARROW - Jump
  • ESC – Pauses the game

GRAPHICAL USER INTERFACE

As far as the GUI is concerned, if we consider
  • Splash Screen – The splash page is simply the main page of the whole game. It contains the name of the game and the logo. It prompts the user to press any button to start. Upon pressing the button, it will briefly explain the background story of the game.
  • Game Menu – After the brief intro the main menu will show up with several buttons to proceed.
    • PLAY – The game will begin.
    • LEVELS – It will move to a page with certain levels to choose.
    • CONTROLS – It will display a screen that includes a description of all game controls.
    • HELP – It will display some information about the game with cheat codes and developer info.
  • In-Game GUI - Once the game begins, the character will be able to be controlled by the user. At every stage, one or a series of instructions/goals would show up to proceed to the next stage. The health bar and pause button would be always displayed at the upper part of the screen.

ARTWORK

All artwork in the game will be original. The following needs to be created:
  • Player - A baseball player who is wearing a baseball uniform. User will playing as this character.
    • Idle
    • Walking Right
    • Walking Left
    • Attacking Right
    • Attacking Left
    • Taking Damage
    • Dying
    • Dead
  • Hitter, pitcher - Monsters in our game. They are wearing baseball uniforms as well.
    • Idle
    • Walking Right
    • Walking Left
    • Attacking Right
    • Attacking Left
    • Taking Damage
    • Dying
    • Dead
  • Background Tiles - The background tiles of a game are depending on the stages. The first stage is the way to the stadium. Second stage is inside the stadium. Third stage is the field. According to them, the first stage will have an image of roads, the second stage will have an image of a stadium, and the third stage will be an image of a field.
  • Floor, Wall Tiles - The player is idle on the floor and there are multiple floating tiles where the player can jump on. Wall tile is the boundary of the player’s movement.
  • Background Tiles - For non-collidable tiles that will go in the background of the chamber, we'll just use a black and white tile set arranged in some pattern.

SOUND EFFECTS

All sound effects will be original. Sounds must be made to coincide with each of the following events:
  • Jumping sound
  • Attacking sound
  • Dying sound
    • Dying sound of player character
    • Dying sound of monsters
  • Taking damage sound
  • Stage clear sound

MUSIC

There will be three different pieces of music added to the game.
  • Main menu/Splash/Tutorial/Stage 1 music
  • Stage 2 music
  • Stage 3 music
