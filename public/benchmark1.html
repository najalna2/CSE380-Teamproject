"Road To The Show"
"Game Design Document"

by Dongha Song, Hangjoon Cho, Yeonsik Kang

Introduction:

His job is to knock out all the other baseball players and claim the victory for himself. He will use physical means to defeat them. His main weapon is his favorite baseball bat, which won't break until all the other baseball players are down. However, the other players won't stand still and watch X take them down. Before they can take down the main character, X will have to take them all down first.

Technology:

Road To The Show is a 2d side scrolling game. The game's construction will be developed for the windows platform using wolfie2d, an engine developed by Richard McKenna. Sound effects will be. Artwork is developed using piskel and krita.

Back Story:

The main character “X” became a baseball player with the determination to win the league. However, he realized that he could never win the league with his skills. He was always fourth. he thought "I want to be number one too. But since I don't have the ability to do that, wouldn't it be possible for me to become number one if there were no superiors?" So he decided to do it.
